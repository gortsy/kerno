About Kerno

Built for filing-heavy workflows

We help equity research teams move from raw SEC text to structured insight — without losing the rigor your PM expects.

Kerno started on a desk that processed forty 10-Ks a week. Analysts were skilled — the bottleneck was format, not judgment.

Today every analysis card carries the same structure: a 3-second take, three metrics, four signals, and a bottom-line summary. That consistency is what lets a team scale coverage without scaling headcount linearly.
